SUPPORTDESK — GENERAL FAQ & TROUBLESHOOTING
===========================================

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
==================

Q: How do I create a new account?
A: Go to https://app.supportdesk.io/signup. Enter your work email, create a password,
   and verify your email address. Your free account is immediately active after verification.

Q: How do I invite team members?
A: From your Dashboard, go to Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter the email addresses
   of your teammates. They will receive an invitation link valid for 48 hours.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address.
   You will receive a password-reset link within 2 minutes. The link expires after 30 minutes.
   If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Q: Can I change my account email address?
A: Yes. Go to Account Settings → Profile → Email. You must verify the new email address.
   The change takes effect after verification. Note: your old email stops working immediately.

Q: How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?
A: Go to Account Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication → Enable.
   Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password).
   Save your backup codes in a secure location.

Q: I'm locked out of my account. What should I do?
A: If 2FA is blocking you: use one of your backup codes. If you've lost backup codes,
   contact support@supportdesk.io from your registered email address for account recovery.
   Recovery typically takes 1–2 business days for identity verification.

INTERFACE & BROWSER ISSUES
===========================

Q: The dashboard isn't loading. What should I do?
A: Try these steps in order:
   1. Clear your browser's cache and cookies (Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data).
   2. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge).
   3. Disable browser extensions, especially ad-blockers or script-blockers.
   4. Check your internet connection.
   5. Visit https://status.supportdesk.io to see if there's an ongoing outage.
   6. If none of the above works, contact support@supportdesk.io with a screenshot.

Q: The interface is loading slowly.
A: Slow loading is usually caused by:
   - A slow internet connection (run a speed test at fast.com)
   - A large number of open browser tabs consuming memory
   - Outdated browser version — update your browser to the latest version
   - Peak usage hours (9 AM – 11 AM EST) — performance typically improves after 11 AM

Q: I see a blank white screen after logging in.
A: This is usually a JavaScript loading error. Steps to fix:
   1. Hard-refresh the page: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac).
   2. Clear all site data in browser settings.
   3. Try Incognito/Private mode.
   4. If the problem persists, report the browser console error to our support team.

Q: Buttons or forms are not responding to clicks.
A: Try disabling browser extensions one by one to identify any conflicts.
   Also ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings.

TICKET MANAGEMENT
==================

Q: How do I create a new support ticket?
A: Click the blue "+ New Ticket" button in the top-right corner of any page.
   Fill in the subject, description, priority, and attach any relevant files.
   Click "Submit Ticket" to create it.

Q: How do I track the status of my ticket?
A: All your tickets are listed under "My Tickets" in the left sidebar.
   Status indicators: Open (blue), In Progress (yellow), Resolved (green), Closed (grey).

Q: How do I reopen a resolved ticket?
A: Open the resolved ticket and click the "Reopen" button at the bottom.
   Add a comment explaining why you're reopening it.

Q: Can I attach files to a ticket?
A: Yes. Supported formats: PDF, PNG, JPG, GIF, MP4, ZIP, CSV, XLSX, DOCX.
   Maximum file size: 25 MB per file, 100 MB total per ticket.

Q: How do I set ticket priority?
A: When creating or editing a ticket, select from: Low, Medium, High, or Critical.
   Critical tickets receive a response within 1 hour during business hours.

INTEGRATIONS
============

Q: Which tools does SupportDesk integrate with?
A: Natively: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Jira, GitHub, Zendesk, Salesforce, HubSpot.
   Via Zapier: 5,000+ additional tools.
   Via API: Any platform with REST API capability.

Q: How do I connect Slack?
A: Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack → Connect.
   Authorize the SupportDesk Slack app in your workspace.
   Choose which channels should receive ticket notifications.

Q: How do I connect Jira?
A: Go to Settings → Integrations → Jira → Connect.
   Enter your Jira domain (e.g., yourcompany.atlassian.net).
   Provide an API token from your Atlassian account.
   Select the Jira project to sync tickets with.

DATA & SECURITY
===============

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Data is stored in ISO 27001-certified data centres in the US (primary) and EU (backup).
   Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3) is applied to all data.

Q: Can I export all my data?
A: Yes. Go to Account Settings → Data → Export All Data.
   You'll receive a download link via email within 2 hours for small accounts,
   or within 24 hours for accounts with more than 10,000 tickets.

Q: Does SupportDesk comply with GDPR?
A: Yes. SupportDesk is fully GDPR-compliant. We offer a Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
   for Enterprise customers. Contact privacy@supportdesk.io to request the DPA.

Q: How do I delete my account and all data?
A: Go to Account Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account.
   All data is permanently deleted within 30 days as per our data retention policy.
   This action is irreversible. Download your data export before deleting.
