SUPPORTDESK BILLING POLICY & FAQ
Version 3.2 | Effective January 2024
=====================================

SECTION 1 — SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
================================

Free Plan
---------
- Up to 3 agents
- 500 tickets/month
- 5 GB storage
- Email support only
- No API access

Standard Plan — $49/agent/month
---------------------------------
- Unlimited tickets
- 50 GB storage
- API access (1,000 req/min)
- Email + chat support
- Webhooks included

Professional Plan — $89/agent/month
-------------------------------------
- Everything in Standard
- 200 GB storage
- API access (5,000 req/min)
- Priority email + chat support
- Custom reporting
- SSO/SAML integration

Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing
----------------------------------
- Everything in Professional
- Unlimited storage
- API access (10,000 req/min)
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone support
- SLA guarantee: 99.9% uptime
- Custom integrations & onboarding

SECTION 2 — BILLING CYCLE
===========================

- All plans are billed monthly or annually.
- Annual billing provides a 20% discount versus monthly.
- Billing date is the same calendar day each month as the original sign-up date.
- Invoices are emailed to the billing contact on the day of charge.
- Payment methods accepted: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Bank Transfer (Enterprise only).

SECTION 3 — UPGRADES & DOWNGRADES
====================================

Upgrading
---------
- Plan upgrades take effect immediately.
- You will be billed a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.
- Example: Upgrading from Standard to Professional on day 15 of a 30-day cycle
  will charge 50% of the price difference for that month.

Downgrading
-----------
- Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
- No prorated refund is issued for unused days in the current cycle.
- Data exceeding the lower plan's limits will be archived (not deleted) for 30 days.

SECTION 4 — REFUND POLICY
===========================

Monthly Subscriptions
----------------------
- Refunds are not issued for partial months.
- If you cancel within 7 days of your first-ever subscription (new accounts only),
  you are eligible for a full refund — this is a one-time courtesy refund.

Annual Subscriptions
---------------------
- You may request a prorated refund for unused full months within the first 30 days
  of an annual subscription start date.
- After 30 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable.

Duplicate Charge Policy
------------------------
- Duplicate charges are fully refunded within 5–7 business days of verification.
- To report a duplicate charge, contact billing@supportdesk.io with your invoice numbers.
- Our billing team will investigate and respond within 1 business day.

SECTION 5 — CANCELLATION
==========================

- You may cancel your subscription at any time from: Account Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- Your data is retained for 30 days post-cancellation, then permanently deleted.
- You will receive a cancellation confirmation email immediately.

SECTION 6 — FAILED PAYMENTS
=============================

- If a payment fails, we will retry 3 times over 7 days.
- You will receive an email notification after each failed attempt.
- If all retries fail, your account will be downgraded to the Free plan.
- No data is lost; upgrading again restores full access.
- Update payment details at: Account Settings → Billing → Payment Method.

SECTION 7 — TAX & VAT
=======================

- Customers in the EU: VAT is applied based on your country of registration.
- If you have a valid VAT ID, add it in Account Settings → Billing → Tax Info to remove VAT.
- US customers: Sales tax applied where required by state law.
- Invoices include a full tax breakdown.

SECTION 8 — BILLING DISPUTE RESOLUTION
=========================================

Timeline
--------
Step 1: Submit dispute via billing@supportdesk.io or the in-app chat.
Step 2: Billing team acknowledges within 1 business day.
Step 3: Investigation completed within 3–5 business days.
Step 4: Resolution communicated via email. Credits or refunds processed within 5–7 business days.

SECTION 9 — ENTERPRISE SLA
============================

- Guaranteed uptime: 99.9% monthly (excludes scheduled maintenance).
- Scheduled maintenance windows: Sundays 2:00 AM – 4:00 AM UTC (notified 48 hours in advance).
- Incident response times:
    Critical (P1) — service down:        Response within 15 minutes
    High (P2) — major feature impaired:  Response within 1 hour
    Medium (P3) — minor issue:           Response within 4 hours
    Low (P4) — question/request:         Response within 1 business day
- SLA credits: 10% of monthly fee per each 0.1% downtime below SLA guarantee.
